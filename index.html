Principal's Message


Dear Students, Parents, and Staff,

I am thrilled to announce that our school has now been newly affiliated with the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE). This is indeed a momentous occasion and a significant step forward in our journey of academic excellence.

With this affiliation, we are now part of a renowned educational system that is recognized nationally and internationally.

It opens up vast opportunities for our students, allowing them to explore new horizons and gain exposure to a broader spectrum of knowledge and learning resources.

While we fondly cherish our past as a state-government-affiliated institution, this transition to CBSE presents us with the chance to embrace progressive methodologies and elevate our educational standards even further.

Together, as a cohesive community, we shall continue to nurture the potential of every student, encouraging their intellectual, emotional, and physical growth. Our dedicated faculty members are committed to providing the best guidance and support to ensure every student's success.

I urge all students to embrace this change with enthusiasm, curiosity, and determination. Let us all work together to make the most of this opportunity, carving a path that leads to academic brilliance and personal growth.

Thank you for your unwavering support and trust in our school. Together, we shall create a future filled with knowledge, compassion, and success.
